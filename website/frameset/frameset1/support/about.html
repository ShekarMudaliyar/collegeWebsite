The Parle Tilak Vidyalaya Association was established in 1921 to commemorate Lokmanya Tilak by undertaking educational activities.
In the past eighty eight years, the institution has grown into a large educational complex with two Primary Schools, two Secondary Schools, one English Medium, one ICSE School, three Junior Colleges, three Degree Colleges and three Post Graduate institutions of Management, the benefit of which is taken by nearly 25,000 students. It can be said with legitimate pride that Parle Tilak Vidyalaya Association has acquired a reputation as one of the leading educational institutions in the state of Maharashtra.
Parle Commerce College was established in June, 1960. After the handsome donation from Shri. M.L.Dahanukar, an industrialist of Mumbai the college was named after him as M.L.Dahanukar College of Commerce in 1962.
The College is affiliated to the University of Mumbai. Colleges has four sections :

  • Junior College
  • Degree College
  • S.F.C. (Self Financing Course)
  • Post-Graduation


Junior College provides instructions to the students of XI and XII. The College is affiliated to University of Mumbai. for the following Three Years Degree Courses.


Bachelor of Commerce with Accounts
Bachelor of Commerce with Management
Bachelor of Management Studies
Bachelor of Commerce (Accounting & Finance)
Bachelor of Commerce (Banking & Insurance)
Bachelor of Science (Information Technology)
Bachelor of Commerce (Financial Markets)
